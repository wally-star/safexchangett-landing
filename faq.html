Frequently Asked Questions

What is SafeXchange TT?

A safer marketplace with escrow payments, verified users (KYC), secure courier delivery, Safe Trade Zones, and dispute support.

How does escrow work?

The buyer pays into escrow. Funds are released to the seller only after delivery is verified by OTP/photo proof. If something goes wrong, disputes are resolved within 48–96 hours.

What are Safe Trade Zones?

Designated public locations for safer in-person exchanges. You can book a time slot and meet under monitored conditions.

Do I have to stop using Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp?

No. Keep using your social platforms—just share your SafeXchange listing link to add escrow and secure delivery.

How much does it cost?

Escrow fees range 1.5–2.5% (min TT$5). Courier fees vary by distance (TT$25–50). Basic browsing is free.
