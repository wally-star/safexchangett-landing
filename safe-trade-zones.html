Safe Trade Zones

Prefer to meet in person? Use a Safe Trade Zone to complete your exchange under safer, monitored conditions.

How it works

  1. Choose “Safe Trade Zone” at checkout.
  2. Select an available location and time slot.
  3. Meet and verify the item or service.
  4. Confirm using OTP, then escrow releases funds.

Planned locations

  • Port of Spain (City Centre)
  • Chaguanas (Central Hub)
  • San Fernando (South Mall)

Note: Availability may vary by day/time. Bring a valid ID. For high-value transactions, we may require Safe Trade Zones or courier-only handover.
