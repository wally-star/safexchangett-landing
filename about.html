About SafeXchange TT

SafeXchange TT is a trust-first marketplace built in Trinidad & Tobago. We combine verified identities, escrow-secured payments, and secure courier handovers with Safe Trade Zones to protect buyers and sellers.

Our Mission

To make everyday trade safe, simple, and transparent—so people can buy and sell with confidence.

  • Local partnership and inclusion
  • Privacy and data protection
