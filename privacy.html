Privacy Policy

SafeXchange TT respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Data Protection Act of Trinidad & Tobago.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data such as your name, email address, and contact details when you register, join our waitlist, or use our platform. We may also collect transaction and delivery information for escrow and courier purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify user identity and prevent fraud.
  • To process transactions and coordinate deliveries.
  • To contact you about your account, security alerts, or service updates.
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use AES-256 encryption, firewalls, and multi-factor authentication (MFA) to protect your information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Sharing

We only share data with partners essential to service delivery (e.g., courier providers, escrow banks) under secure agreements. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@safexchangett.com.
